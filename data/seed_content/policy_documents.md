# Policy Documents

## Refund Policy
**Category:** Financing
**Risk Level:** High
**Intent:** Decision
**Answer Type:** Policy

SynergisticIT offers a refund period for the initial deposit. If a candidate decides to withdraw within the first 1 week of the training program, they are eligible for a partial refund minus administrative fees. After the first week, the deposit is non-refundable.

## ISA Payment Terms & Conditions
**Category:** Financing
**Risk Level:** High
**Intent:** Decision
**Answer Type:** Policy

1. **Activation**: ISA payments activate only when the candidate secures a position with a gross annual salary of $81,000 or more.
2. **Monthly Payments**: The candidate agrees to pay a fixed percentage (typically 10-15%) of their gross monthly income.
3. **Payment Pause**: Payments are paused if the candidate becomes unemployed or their income drops below the threshold.
4. **Term limit**: The ISA obligation ends after the agreed-upon number of monthly payments are made OR the maximum payment cap is reached, whichever comes first.

## Employment Classification & Support
**Category:** Placement
**Risk Level:** Medium
**Intent:** Decision
**Answer Type:** Policy

Candidates placed through SynergisticIT may be classified as W2 employees of SynergisticIT working at client sites, or directly hired by clients, depending on the specific contract. SynergisticIT provides ongoing support during the initial employment period to ensure retention and performance.

## Placement Guarantee Disclaimer
**Category:** Placement
**Risk Level:** High
**Intent:** Decision
**Answer Type:** Policy

SynergisticIT does NOT guarantee employment. Employment success depends on market conditions, candidate effort, and successful completion of the training and interview preparation modules. "Guarantee" in marketing materials refers to our commitment to continue supporting the candidate indefinitely until placement is achieved, provided the candidate fulfills their obligations (attendance, applications, responsiveness).
